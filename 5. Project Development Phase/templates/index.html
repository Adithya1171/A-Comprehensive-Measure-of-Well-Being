THE THREE PILLARS OF HDI

The core dimensions used to compute the Human Development Index score.

Long and Healthy Life

Measured by **Life Expectancy at Birth**, assessing the overall longevity, healthcare infrastructure, and quality of life of a nation's citizenry.

Access to Knowledge

Measured by **Expected Years of Schooling** for children and **Mean Years of Schooling** for adults aged 25 and older, indicating educational standards and literacy rates.

Decent Standard of Living

Measured by **Gross National Income (GNI) per Capita** (purchasing power parity, PPP$), evaluating average economic productivity and financial well-being.
